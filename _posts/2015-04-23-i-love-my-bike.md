At Clearleft we implemented a Cycle to Work scheme a year or so ago. Which gave just about everyone an excuse to get a new bike, whether or not they were already cycling to work.

We have a a fair few cycling enthusiasts here so opinions and recommendations were as varied as they were plentiful. In the end, after much online research, I plumped for the [Boardman Team 650b](http://www.halfords.com/cycling/bikes/mountain-bikes/boardman-mountain-bike-team-ht-650b) which rather unfortunately can only be bought from Halfords (whose reputation for awful customer service is entirely justified, btw).

Instagram tells me that I bought it ten months ago.
